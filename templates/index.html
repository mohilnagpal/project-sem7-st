Who we are?

StockedIT is a platform that aims to guide and ease the investment journey for novice investors. We aim to provide financial literacy to our users and recommend good-performing stocks purely on the basis of their fundamental analysis. With StockedIt instead of investing in charts, we will be investing in businesses. It will dig deep into the companyâ€™s financials to display the best and most accurate results to our users. These stocks if considered for long-term investing will definitely give great results. For those who are starting their stock investment journey, we have developed readymade portfolios, and for those experienced stock players, StockedIT will help reduce the time and effort required in individual stock research. Currently, we are working on 25 sectors of the market with more than 100+ stocks and 3 diversified portfolios.

What we do?

Currently, in India, we have very few investment options left where we can expect an annual return of more than 8% considering the high inflation rate of 6%. Any investment made which gives less than 6% annual return implies burning your hard-earned savings. The stock market is the only viable investment option left where we can expect a minimum of 7-10% annual returns and there is no upper limit to these returns. StockedIT makes your investment journey effortless and improves the financial literacy of its users so that they can take calculative and confident risks without giving hefty fees to their brokers. StockedIT aims to simplify the fundamental analysis of all the stocks of various sectors available in the market and also generate a diversified portfolio for long-term risk-free investments.

How it works?

StockedIT focuses on the fundamental analysis of different stocks to suggest good performing stocks for long-term and short-term investments for high returns. The stocks will go through indicators such as reserves and surplus, debt to equity ratio, price to earnings ratio, valuation of the stock, Earnings per share, return on equity, etc. The performance on these indicators will be measured in the form of StockedIT points. The stocks in each sector will be ranked on the basis of these points. StockedIt works on real-time data to give the most accurate results to its users. Investors can pick the top stocks from each sector to create a strong low-risk portfolio and fulfill their financial goals. On the basis of these rankings, weâ€™ll generate a readymade portfolio to ease the investing process for novice investors.
